About Me

Design Operations

I’ve worked at two growth-stage start ups, hired both times as design is still starting out as a function in these companies. In this unsurprisingly ambiguous environment, I’ve found myself drawn to design operations work. As I saw areas to improve my own design quality and efficiency, I realized all our designers would benefit from structure and began defining processes. I’m particularly interested in measuring design quality, scaling design, design hiring and breaking down work silos.
