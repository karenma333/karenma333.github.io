Problem

Following a series of need-finding interviews, we used an affinity diagram to focus in on the number of tools needed to be used. However, we later learned that this was actually just a symptom of the real problem, budgeting. For many college students, budget is the limiting factor for everything when it comes to traveling (flights, hotels, etc.). Students in particular donâ€™t really have a disposable income, so their budget is smaller and becomes a real obstacle to traveling.

Solution

To solve this problem, we came up with the idea of creating a Google extension. This would let users see how much they are literally using on their trip. By having a Google extension, this budget could follow the user from each travel website they use. We also wanted to include budget estimators since students did not know how much they should be spending. Budgets would be categorized by different levels of spending profiles so they could be adjusted accordingly.
