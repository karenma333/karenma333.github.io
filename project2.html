Design Process

After some initial needfinding interviews, we realized that while a lot of people show interest in events, when the day of the event comes, people don't want to go. This is due to a few main factors: lack of transportation, social anxiety of not knowing anyone there, and lack of will to resolve these issues by themselves. From here, we concluded that we wanted to create a ridesharing application that is centered around eventgoing. Given the time constraints of the course, we drew out a brief mapping of all the screens we deemed necessary before going straight into development.
