UCPool: Mobile Web Interaction Design

Overview

Role/Tools

I focused on needfinding, creating initial wireframes to guide development and front-end coding as well as conducted usability testing with Google Analytics.
Tools: HTML/CSS, Google Analytics, Javascript

Problem

When looking at Facebook events, there are often hundreds of people interested in events but the actual turnout is much lower. We wanted to see if we could improve attendance rates at events.

Solution

We decided to improve ridesharing for events. Even though events are interesting, people are uncomfortable going to events when they do not know anyone and they don't want to go through the hassle of finding a way to get there. We created a mobile web application that pulled events that you were interested in on Facebook. Then you could either offer to drive or find a ride for each event. After inputting your pick-up location, we would find the most efficient route for carpooling purposes. We would also take into account mutual friends and Facebook interests in the hopes that the people you carpool with can be people you socialize with at the events themselves.
